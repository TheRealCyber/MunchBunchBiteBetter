Our Journey

Our journey began as a group of computer science and business systems students at NMIMS, united by a shared passion for technology and health. As we delved into our studies, we recognized a gap in the market: a lack of accessible, personalized tools to help individuals make better food choices. Inspired by the growing need for health-conscious solutions, we set out to create an app that could bridge this gap. From brainstorming ideas to developing our app, we focused on building a platform that combines technology, user-centric design, and nutrition science. We wanted to empower people—especially those with specific health goals, like pregnant women, new mothers, or those with allergies—to make smarter, informed decisions about their food. Our app uses AI to analyze food products and offer tailored recommendations, making it easier for users to understand how each product fits into their lifestyle. Through countless hours of coding, testing, and refining, we’ve built BiteBetter to be a reliable, easy-to-use health companion. Today, we’re proud to see our vision come to life, and we remain committed to continuously improving our platform to help more people lead healthier, happier lives.
