Scan Product Barcode

1. Download the IP Webcam app from the Play Store.
2. Navigate to Service Control and tap Start Server.
3. At the bottom of the screen, note down the address (up to 137:8080).
4. Make sure:
You are in a well-lit room.
Your phone's camera is properly aligned with the product's barcode.
Hold your phone steady until the barcode is successfully scanned.
Ensure that both your phone and the device are connected to the same network.
